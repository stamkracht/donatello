8.1.1 Alignment

Aligns your text. When aligning inside a table layout the following utilities are also possible.

  • u-align-top
  • u-align-bottom
  • u-align-middle

Example

Left

Center

Right

Justify

<p class="u-align-left">Left</p>
<p class="u-align-center">Center</p>
<p class="u-align-right">Right</p>
<p class="u-align-justify">Justify</p>
