3.3 Reset margin tags

Sets the margins to 0 for the following elements:

  • hr
  • ul, ol
  • dl, dd
  • table,
  • form, fieldset
  • figure
  • address
  • blockquote
  • p
  • h1, h2, h3, h4, h5, h6
